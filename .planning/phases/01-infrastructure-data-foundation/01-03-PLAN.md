         - Constructor takes packManager reference
         - `initialize(): Promise<void>` -- query installed nutrition packs, open NutritionService for each
         - Internal state: `databases: Map<string, NutritionService>` keyed by pack ID, `priority: string[]` (ordered list of pack IDs to query)
         - Priority ordering: regional packs first (AFCD, CoFID, CIQUAL in order of installation), then USDA core, then USDA branded (if installed)
         - `searchFoods(query: string, limit?: number): Promise<ResolvedFood[]>`:
